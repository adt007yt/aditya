Que.1What is the National Environment Science Camp all about?

Ans. The National Environment Science Camp is nationwide movement for Environment Conservation. Campers from schools all over India assemble at Dudhwa National Park to Observe, Experience and Conserve the pristine Environment.

Que.3.What exactly is Karavan Heritage and Nature Society?

Ans. Karavan Heritage and Nature Society is a 20 year old non-profit organization devoted to Environment Education and Conservation.

Que.4. Which is the nearest airport from Dudhwa National Park?

Ans. The nearest operating airport is Lucknow.

Que.5.What would be the right age group for the Camp?

National Environment Science Camp is an effort to correlate the theoretical knowledge with the real world outdoors. We have designed hands on activities to interest all age groups (especially Students from Class Fourth to Class Twelfth). The Scientific content is modified accordingly.

Que.6. What do my students gain from your Camp?

Ans.The participants, camping right in the heart of a jungle, away from the luxuries and distractions of metro-life, automatically realize the importance of the Environment, of Community living and optimum utilization of the resources. Just after four days of camping experience, they emerge a better person.The Camp equips the Participants with Environmental Values, Scientific Knowledge and Soft Skills:- Appreciation of Nature, – Conservation ethics, Conceptual understanding of the Environment and Ecology, Experimental approach, Ability to observe, think and analyze, Tribal espirit d’corps, Self Discipline and Confidence, Rugged, let’s rough it out attitude.
